Our Story

From a garage in San Francisco to a global brand

In 2015, our founders Marcus Chen and Lena Voss were frustrated by the trade-off between performance and design in consumer laptops. They set out to build something different — machines that didn't compromise.

The first Folio prototype was assembled in a 400 sq ft garage in the Mission District. Within six months, 200 units had shipped to early adopters who spread the word.

Today, Folio's 140-person team designs every laptop in-house — from the chassis architecture to the firmware — with a singular obsession: making the best possible tool for the people who use it every day.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

⚡
Uncompromising Performance
We refuse to ship anything that doesn't perform at the top of its class. Every chip, every SSD, every display is selected with this standard in mind.
🎨
Intentional Design
Great design isn't decoration — it's function made beautiful. We obsess over every detail, from hinge torque to key travel to the exact weight of the chassis.
🌍
Built to Last
In a world of disposable electronics, we build machines meant to last 5–7 years. Our 2-year warranty backs up that commitment.
🤝
Customer First
Every decision we make starts with one question: does this make life better for the person using our product? If the answer isn't yes, we don't ship it.
🔬
Relentless Innovation
We don't follow trends — we set them. Our R&D team works two years ahead, so when new technology matures, we're ready to ship it right.
🌱
Responsible Business
By 2027, all Folio packaging will be 100% recycled. We offset all shipping emissions and donate 1% of revenue to digital literacy programs.
